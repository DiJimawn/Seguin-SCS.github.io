History about Seguin Computer Science Club

Computer Science Club

 We are builders. Dreamers. Problem-solvers. Tinkerers. We are Seguin Computer Science Club (SCSC), the first one and only computer science organization on campus. Our mission is to grow and strengthen the student developer community here at Seguin High School and connect our members to all the people, resources, and opportunities they want to achieve their goals. This upcoming year, we're launching a lot of exciting programs and would love for you to join us!
